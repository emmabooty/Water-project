About Charity: Water

Charity: Water is a non-profit organization that brings clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. Founded in 2006, the organization has funded over 60,000 water projects in 29 countries, providing millions of people with access to clean water.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that everyone has access to clean and safe drinking water. We believe that water is a basic human right, and we work tirelessly to make that a reality for communities in need.

How You Can Help

You can make a difference by donating to our cause or spreading the word about our initiatives. Every contribution counts and helps us reach more people in need of clean water.
